Child development Features

Helps wisely spend money on child development by providing a guide with activity and toys for every growth stage, based on child's capabilities and the skill parent want to develop.

Today

Schedule of activities, reminders, and an article recommendation. Selected activity redirects to the activity page, which contains information about it.
Weekly plan

Scheduled activities and topics, list of necessary materials for the week, that can be printed, downloaded as a pdf, and shared.
Skills and Milestones

Milestone checklist based on child age. Skills that should be developed, based on the child's age
Child Health History Features

Record all relevant health information, such as medical history, disease symptoms, allergies, medications, and other important details, and organize it by categories and sub-categories. Uploading medical files and organizing them by category.
Treatments&Medicines

Tracking treatments. Create treatment plans and reminders. Tracking medicines, including information, link and expiration date.
Growth&Health

Tracking growth measures: height, weight, head circumference.
Vaccines: program, tracking.
Doctors&Appointments

Doctors contact.
Appointments, that will appear as reminders in today feed.
